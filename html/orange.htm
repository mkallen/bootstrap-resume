Member of The North Mississippi Software Developer's Group

August 2010 to Present

The North Mississippi Software Developer’s Group is a community of local developers. The group isn’t specific to a particular platform, technology, or programming language and is intended to be as inclusive as possible. The purpose of the group is to bring local developers together in order to network, share experiences, and learn about new technologies.
